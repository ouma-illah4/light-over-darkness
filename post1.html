Life isn’t always bright. There are moments when darkness feels heavier than the air itself — when you can’t see tomorrow clearly. But even in such times, one truth remains unshaken: the light of Christ still shines.

Darkness doesn’t mean God is absent. Sometimes, it’s the very space where faith learns to breathe again. In your tears, prayers, and stillness, God is working unseen — shaping hope, refining your heart, and teaching you to see differently.

“The light shines in the darkness, and the darkness has not overcome it.” – John 1:5

You might not feel strong, but grace doesn’t depend on your strength — it flows through surrender. The moment you stop trying to carry it all alone, light begins to break through.

Keep holding on. Even the longest night ends when the sun rises — and in Christ, dawn is guaranteed.
